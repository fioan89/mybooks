About MyBooks

				MyBooks is an open project: the software is open, the data are open,
				the documentation is open, and we welcome your contribution. Whether you fix a typo, 
				or add a book--it's all welcome.MyBooks it's founded as a wrapper around OpenLibrary
				and much of the data found on this website is taken from OpenLibrary data dump's.  
				
				So thanks OpenLibrary and let's not forget the nonprofit Internet Archive guys.
